# Data Deletion Instructions

Manage My n8n Node 2 does not store personal user data in its own database.

To remove the app’s access to your Meta account:
1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
2. Find “Manage My n8n Node 2”
3. Click Remove
